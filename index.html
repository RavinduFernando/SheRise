User Journey – From Learning to Income

A typical SheRise user follows a clear, guided path from sign‑up to real income, with support at every step.

1

Step 1

Sign‑Up & Profile

She creates a profile on her phone, shares her education, skills, and goals – job, freelance, or business – so SheRise can recommend the right pathway.

2

Step 2

Skills & Learning Path (SheLearn)

She follows short, local‑language courses such as “Intro to Digital Marketing”, “Selling Online”, or “Personal Budgeting” to build confidence and market‑ready skills.

3

Step 3

Economic Activation (SheWork / SheGrow)

Depending on her goal, she is matched to jobs and freelance projects, or guided to build a simple business plan and connect with partner microfinance institutions and banks.

4

Step 4

Support & Growth (SheConnect)

She joins community groups and mentoring circles, continues advanced courses, and steadily grows her income and impact.

Market Validation – What Women Told Us

We surveyed 20 Sri Lankan women aged 19–40 to understand their realities, goals, and willingness to use a platform like SheRise.

Income & Goals

Only 20% of women in our sample have a stable income. 75% either earn irregularly or do not earn but want to. About 50% are job‑focused, while 45% want to start or grow a business.

40% not earning but want to
35% irregular income
30% want full‑time jobs
25% want to grow a business

Key Barriers

The top barriers named were finance (35%), skills gaps (30%), and lack of opportunities (25%). Most women do not feel confident about money or business decisions.

Platform Interest & Pricing

90% of respondents are very or somewhat interested in a SheRise‑type platform. Only 15% said courses must be free; most are comfortable in the LKR 1,000–3,000 range for short courses.

55% very interested
35% somewhat interested
45% can pay LKR 1,001–3,000
Most prefer < LKR 500 / month for premium

These insights confirm both the social need and the commercial potential for SheRise – especially with a mix of free access, scholarships, and affordable premium tiers.

About SheRise & Partnerships
